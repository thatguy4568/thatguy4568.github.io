About Me

Good day! My name is Norm Miller. I am a rising senior majoring in Data Science and pursuing an academic minor in History at CUNY New York City College of Technology (City Tech). Last semester, I participated in the Honors Scholars Program, completing an honors credit in a regular course for my ENG 2142 Poetry class. We develop proposals to benefit the public interest of New Yorkers across the state. If you’d like to learn more about me, please don’t hesitate to contact me.
